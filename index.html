Operating Instructions

  1. Key ZK: Turn the key to ON to activate the driver's cab.
  2. Reverser Handle: Defaults to Neutral "N". Select "Forward F" to move forward. "Reverse R" is used for restricted speed reverse (ATO only allows F). Reversing is only allowed when completely stopped.
  3. Door Interlock: Traction is cut off when any door opens, simulating real train physics.
  4. Cab Key ZK=OFF: The console and highlights dim (indicating unassigned/sleep). Ambient sounds require clicking the console first due to browser audio policies.
  5. Modes: Defaults to RM (Restricted Manual, max 25 km/h). Use "Mode Up" to advance to CM (ATP Manual) → AM (ATO Auto) → FAM (Fully Automatic).
  6. Master Controller: Drag or use WS keys. Up is traction, down is service brake, bottom is rapid brake (EB).
  7. ATO and Startup Permission: The small light on ATO Start flashes only when startup is permitted; the light turns off during ATO driving. For M/M, A/M, ATO exits upon arrival; after alighting and boarding, when the light flashes again, you must press the button. For A/A, it engages automatically if conditions are met.
  8. Door Enable: After an accurate stop in AM/FAM, ATP only issues door enable for the platform side. Pressing Door Enable manually permits doors on both sides (CM/debugging).
  9. ATO Mode: Outputs traction/braking commands, integrating vehicle response and resistance for position/speed. After accurate stopping, ATP enables/opens platform doors based on the door mode.
  10. Door Mode: (Active only for AM/FAM auto stops): After stopping, ATP enables platform doors; M/M requires manual opening; A/M auto opens; A/A auto opens/closes and departs.
  11. Max Authorized Mode: Determines the max authorized mode in DMI Zone 5; differs from the current mode (Zone 13). Mode upgrades cannot exceed this limit.
  12. EB: Overspeeding, the emergency button, or open doors trigger emergency braking. Reset the handle to zero after stopping to release.
